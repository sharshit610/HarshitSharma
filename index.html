About Me

I am a PhD Scholar in Economics at Department of Economic Science, IIT Kanpur. I am currently pursuing my doctoal degree under the supervision of Dr. Wasim Ahmad. My research interest lies at the intersection of Macroeconomics, Banking & Finance, and Complex Network Analysis, with a particular interest in Systemic Risk and Financial Stability. Broadly, my work explores how systemic risk and interconnectedness influence the resilience and dynamics of financial institutions and markets.

I completed my M.Sc. in Economics from IIT Roorkee, and B.Sc. in Mathematics from Govt. NPG College of Science Raipur.

Beyond academics, I enjoy cricket, singing, and cooking.
