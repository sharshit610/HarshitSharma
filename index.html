About Me

I am a Ph.D. Candidate in Economics at IIT Kanpur. My research focuses on Macroeconomics, Banking and Finance, and Complex Network Analysis, with a particular interest in Systemic Risk and Financial Stability. My work explores how systemic risk and interconnectedness shape financial institutions and markets.

I completed my M.Sc. in Economics from IIT Roorkee, and B.Sc. in Mathematics from Govt. NPG College of Science Raipur.

Beyond academics, I enjoy cricket, singing, and cooking.
